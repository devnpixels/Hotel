RESPONSIBLE DISCLOSURE At HoteliPad, we take responsible disclosure very seriously.

We understand that security vulnerabilities can potentially compromise the safety and privacy of our customers, and we want to ensure that any such vulnerabilities are identified and addressed as quickly as possible. That's why we encourage responsible disclosure from security researchers and other individuals who discover potential vulnerabilities in our system. If you believe you have identified a security vulnerability in our platform, we ask that you please contact us immediately so that we can investigate the issue and take appropriate action. We are committed to working with security researchers in a responsible and transparent manner to resolve any potential vulnerabilities, and we will provide updates and communicate openly throughout the disclosure process. At HoteliPad, we believe that responsible disclosure is a key component of maintaining the trust and safety of our customers, and we are dedicated to working with the security community to ensure the ongoing security and integrity of our platform.
